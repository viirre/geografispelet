This is a list of the 77 [[Italy|Italian]] [[DOCG]] (''denominazione di origine controllata e garantita'') wines ordered by region.<ref>{{cite web |title=Disciplinari dei vini DOP e IGP italiani |url=https://www.politicheagricole.it/flex/cm/pages/ServeBLOB.php/L/IT/IDPagina/4625 |publisher=Ministero delle politiche agricole alimentari e forestali |access-date=26 November 2019}}</ref> The four original DOCGs were [[Brunello di Montalcino|Brunello]], [[Vino Nobile]], and [[Barolo]] (all approved by a presidential decree in July 1980) and [[Barbaresco]] (as approved in October 1980).
